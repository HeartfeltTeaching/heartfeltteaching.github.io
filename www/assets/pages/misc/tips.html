Choices in the Classroom Setting

"You can play with the doll together, or I can figure out another toy for you to play with."

"You may turn in the assignment at the end of class or at the end of the day."

"Would you like to read the book together or with the audiobooks?"

"Would you like to present your project on a poster or computer?"

"You may work with a partner or choose to work independently."

"You may work at your desk or on the beanbag."

"You may choose to type or write out your responses."

""You may choose to do the odd problems first  or the work even."

"You may choose a partner or have the teacher pick one for you."

"Your book report can be presented either orally or written."

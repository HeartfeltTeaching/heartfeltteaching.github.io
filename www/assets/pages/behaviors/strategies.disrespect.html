When dealing with disrespectful students, remember to avoid the power struggle. Allow time to pass before addressing the issue. Take time to breathe and relax.

  • Use very few words- Using fewer words shows your students that you are not getting drawn into their trap for a battle.
  • Follow through with a consequence that is appropriate for you and your classroom.
  • Drive your statements with empathy- offer kind words before delivering a consequence to build a respectful relationship with your students
